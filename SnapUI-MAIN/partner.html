Register and create your profile

First you need to register, create your profile and list the services that you provide. Our team will help you create profiles to spike your customer conversion.

Start receiving funeral requests

Our funeral home partners in the customers’ area receive their request in real-time and can immediately submit an arrangement proposal to the client.

Customer reviews funeral proposals

The customer reviews the proposals they’ve received from funeral homes and views their profiles in order to decide on their match.

User accepts proposal

Users choose the funeral home they believe best represents their wishes and needs. Upon their selection, the user is charged and you are notified to start making the funeral arrangements.
